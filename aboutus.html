Our Story

A visionary team embarked on a journey seven years ago, driven by the mission to connect top-tier tech talent with organizations in need of cutting-edge solutions. Thus, HIRE ME was born, a trailblazing company dedicated to shaping the future of technology across America and Europe.
Our team of dedicated professionals has scoured the tech hubs, identifying and recruiting the brightest minds in software development, data science, artificial intelligence, and beyond.

Why We Started & Where We Are Now

Microsystems acquires XRef, and evolves its market-leading proofreading solution, while strengthening the company’s UK presence. Microsystems opens its third office in New York City. Avaneesh Marwaha is named CEO of Microsystems.

Microsystems, The Sackett Group, and HireMe Corp. merge to create HireMe Microsystems, with a portfolio of market-leading products for document drafting. Microsystems relocates its global headquarters to downtown Chicago. Microsystems exceeds 300 firms globally using Contract Companion.

HireMe acquires Workshare and Doxly, creating a leading, global supplier of document drafting and transaction management technology. HireMe launches HireMe Connect, its third-party technology partner program..
